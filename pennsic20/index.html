A Summary of Dancing at Pennsic 20

... as remembered by Mistresss Rosina:

Master Sion Andreas was in charge of dance. He had different people proctor on different evenings. There was a DMs meeting Mon 7-8 PM Ball was Fri Aug 16. A Canadian film crew, not in garb, got in the way. Rostiboli and Il Canario got done as performances during the breaks.

Classes ran from Mon Aug 12 to Sat Aug 16, 103 were listed in the booklet, Held in 3 class tents and the bathhouse.

Dance Classes listed:

  • Thu 3-5 = 15th Century Dance, Master Niall McKennett (I believe he taught Pellegrina)
  • Sat 9-10:30 = Scottish Dance, Lord Daibhaidh
  • Sat 1-2 = Basic Arbeau Bransles, Lord Edouard d?Aubigny
Dance class not listed, as I added it when I saw how few dance classes there were:

  • Weds 4-6 = Colonesse + Il Canario, Roswitha (now known as Rosina)
