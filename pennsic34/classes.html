"What sorts of class proposals are you looking for?

    Schedule to be done on May 1st, until then here are the kinds of classes for which I'm looking:

  • Classes on all sorts of pre-17th century and SCA-compatible English Country dances, the more variety the better! There should be a good mix of easy dances and advanced dances, since dancers of all skill levels attend. If you would like to do a focused-beginner class for a specific dance genre, or an absolute beginner class, please contact the Dance Coordinator directly. Last year there was a children's dance class that was a big success, and we would like to have more this year.
  • Classes on the popular favorites that are done often at Pennsic, and also new dances that have not been taught at Pennsic.
  • Classes on dance movement and style rather than dance choreographies. The period sources described how to be a graceful dancer, and classes on this topic have been a success in years past.
  • Classes on reconstructing dances from the period sources, and ones that go into the history of dance.
